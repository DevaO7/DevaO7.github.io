Blog

I am a final year Dual Degree (Bachelor’s and Master’s) student pursuing Electrical Engineering at IIT Madras. I have a deep interest in understanding things and enjoy raising and answering fundamental questions, not just limited to STEM but also on themes like human behavior, society, culture, ethics, etc. I believe this quality has led me to embark on a journey to pursue research!

I am currently working on the Low Light Image Enhancement problem (in simple terms, making dark images bright!) under Dr. Kaushik Mitra for my Master’s thesis. In the summer of 2023, I worked on devising a Reinforcement Learning based smart energy management system for households under the guidance of Dr. Martin Takac. I am currently interested in exploring Machine Learning theory and looking for a research assistantship/internship in the same.

Apart from academics and research, I love going for runs and enjoy thinking and jotting down my thoughts on various topics! Feel free to check them on the Blog page
