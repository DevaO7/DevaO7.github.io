Hi! I am Devaganthan :)

I’m a second-year Master’s student in Machine Learning Department at MBZUAI, Abu Dhabi, where I’m lucky to be advised by Prof. Samuel Horvath and Prof. Praneeth Vepakomma

I also love running and am drawn to all things artistic, especially film and music.
